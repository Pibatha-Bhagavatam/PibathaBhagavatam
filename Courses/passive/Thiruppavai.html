Course Description

Thiruppavai is a set of 30 devotional hymns composed by the revered poet-saint Andal, one of the twelve Alvars of Tamil Bhakti tradition. These hymns are sung with deep reverence during the sacred Margazhi (Dhanur) month and are considered an important part of Vaishnavism. This course will introduce students to the profound meanings, poetic beauty, and spiritual essence of Thiruppavai.

In this course, students will explore each verse in detail, learning its pronunciation, meaning, and historical significance. Special emphasis will be given to musical rendition, ensuring that learners not only understand the verses but also learn to sing them with proper intonation and devotion. Suitable for both beginners and those looking to deepen their knowledge, this course provides a structured way to engage with Andal's timeless work.
