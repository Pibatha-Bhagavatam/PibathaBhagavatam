Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age requirement for joining the classes? ▼
There is no strict age limit, but a basic ability to follow instructions is required.
Do I need any prior music experience? ▼
No, beginners are welcome. We start from the basics.
Which language is used for instruction? ▼
Classes are conducted in English and Tamil/Malayalam as needed.
Do I need any instruments to start? ▼
While instruments can be helpful, vocal classes do not require them.
Are classes conducted online? ▼
Yes, all classes are conducted via Google Meet.
