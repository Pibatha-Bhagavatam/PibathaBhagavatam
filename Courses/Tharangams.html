Tharangams

Course Description

Tharangams, composed by the saint-poet Narayana Teertha, are a collection of devotional songs dedicated to Lord Krishna. These compositions are known for their rhythmic beauty, lyrical excellence, and spiritual depth. Each Tharangam is set to melodious Carnatic ragas, making them an integral part of devotional music and Bharatanatyam dance performances.

In this course, students will explore selected Tharangams, learning their meaning, pronunciation, and musical compositions. Emphasis will be placed on correct swara patterns, lyrical interpretation, and devotional expression. This course is suitable for anyone interested in Bhakti music, classical singing, or exploring the richness of Narayana Teertha’s compositions.

Key Details

  • Difficulty Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Course Duration: 10 Weeks
  • Instructor: Experienced Carnatic Musicians
  • Mode: Online (Live)
  • Class Frequency: Twice a Week
  • Learning Material: PDF Notes & Audio Recordings
  • Assessment: Recitation & Musical Exercises
  • Pre-requisites: Basic knowledge of Carnatic music is helpful
  • Languages: Sanskrit & Tamil
  • Certificate: Provided upon completion
