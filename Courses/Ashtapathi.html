Course Description

GITA GOVINDAM

Gita Govindam – Learn the 24 Ashtapathis of Jayadeva Immerse yourself in the divine poetry of Jayadeva through his 24 Ashtapathis, enriched with slokams and namavalis. This course offers: 🎵 Raga-based teaching with exercises 📚 Step-by-step, easy-to-follow lessons 📺 On-screen notations for clarity 🕰 Learn anytime, at your own pace and comfort 📅 Choose monthly access or full course purchase Perfect for lovers of devotional music and seekers of lyrical beauty.

Key Details

  • Difficulty Level: Beginner to Advanced
  • Course Duration: 10 Weeks
  • Instructor: Experienced Sanskrit Scholars & Musicians
  • Mode: Online (Live)
  • Class Frequency: Weekly Sessions
  • Learning Material: PDF Notes & Audio Recordings
  • Assessment: Recitation & Music Practice Sessions
  • Pre-requisites: No prior experience required
  • Languages: Sanskrit & English
  • Certificate: Provided upon completion

Platform

Live sessions will be conducted via Google Meet.
